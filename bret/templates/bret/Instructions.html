Bomb Risk Elicitation Task

You will see a grid of {{ Constants.num_boxes }} boxes (5 × 5). One of these boxes contains a bomb — but you won't know which one!

Click on boxes to collect them. Each collected box is worth {{ Constants.points_per_box }} points.

Strategy tip: The more boxes you open, the more points you can earn — but also the greater the chance you'll hit the bomb!
