Balloon Analogue Risk Task

In this task you will see {{ Constants.num_rounds }} balloons, one at a time. For each balloon, you can pump it up to earn money.

Each pump adds ${{ Constants.pump_value }} to your potential earnings for that balloon. But be careful — each pump also increases the chance that the balloon will pop!

If the balloon pops, you lose all the money you earned for that balloon. If you click "Collect $$$" before it pops, you keep the money and move on to the next balloon.

The balloon can pop at any point from the first pump up to pump {{ Constants.max_pumps }}. You won't know exactly when it will pop — the risk increases with each pump.

Your total earnings are the sum of what you collect across all {{ Constants.num_rounds }} balloons.

Strategy tip: Balance risk and reward. More pumps = more potential money, but also more risk of popping!
